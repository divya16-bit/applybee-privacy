1. Introduction

ApplyBee ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

When you use ApplyBee, we collect the following information:

2.1 Personally Identifiable Information

  • Resume Content: When you upload your resume, we extract and process information including:
    • Your name (first and last name)
    • Email address
    • Phone number
    • Work experience and skills
    • Education history
    • Other information present in your resume

2.2 Location Information

  • City, state, and country information extracted from your resume
  • Used for auto-filling location fields in job applications

2.3 Website Content

  • Job descriptions and requirements from Greenhouse.io job posting pages
  • Form field labels and structure from job application pages
  • Only collected when you are actively using the extension on Greenhouse.io pages

2.4 User Activity

  • Form field interactions on Greenhouse.io job application pages (only when you click "Autofill Form")
  • This is user-initiated and not passive monitoring

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Resume Scoring: To calculate compatibility scores between your resume and job descriptions
  • Auto-Fill Functionality: To automatically populate job application forms with your information
  • Form Field Matching: To identify and fill appropriate fields in application forms
  • Service Improvement: To improve the accuracy and functionality of our extension

4. Data Storage

4.1 Local Storage

Your resume data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's local storage API. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us unless you explicitly use our features.

4.2 Backend Processing

When you click "Get Resume Score" or "Autofill Form", your resume data and job description URLs are sent to our secure backend API at applybee.up.railway.app for processing. This data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted connection).

4.3 Data Retention

Local Data: Your resume data is stored in your browser until you uninstall the extension or clear your browser data.
Backend Data: We do not permanently store your resume data on our servers. Data is processed in real-time and not retained after processing completes.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT:

  • Sell, rent, or trade your personal information
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Track your browsing behavior on websites other than Greenhouse.io

Your data is only sent to our secure backend API (applybee.up.railway.app) for processing when you explicitly request features (clicking "Get Resume Score" or "Autofill Form").

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Data is stored locally in your browser (under your control)
  • Backend API uses secure connections and authentication
  • We follow industry best practices for data security

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View the data stored in your browser (via Chrome storage)
  • Delete: Remove all extension data by uninstalling the extension or clearing browser storage
  • Control: Only use features when you explicitly click buttons (no automatic data collection)
  • Opt-out: Stop using the extension at any time

8. Third-Party Services

Our extension integrates with:

  • Greenhouse.io: We only access Greenhouse.io pages when you are actively using the extension. We do not have any partnership or data sharing agreement with Greenhouse.
  • Our Backend API: Hosted on Railway (applybee.up.railway.app) for resume processing and scoring.

9. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
